Who we are

A legacy of trust since 1948

Established in 1948, Bharucha & Co. stands as one of the oldest law firms in Pakistan. Across more than seven decades, the firm has built an enduring reputation for diligence, discretion, and depth of counsel.

We offer a comprehensive range of intellectual property services — including patents, trademarks, designs, and copyright — alongside extensive, well-rounded advisory services spanning the many facets of civil and corporate law.
